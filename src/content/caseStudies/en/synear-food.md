## Project Overview

Synear Food is a leading enterprise in China's frozen food industry. The Suiping factory steamed dumpling production line had 21 forming machines using manual filling — inefficient, labor-intensive, and prone to downtime. Borica deployed 2 automatic filling systems with ZGL-V3.0 smart scheduling.

## Customer Value

- **Efficiency**: Automated cart refilling, 2.5 tons/h per line, near-zero downtime
- **Food Safety**: Fully enclosed, food-grade design passed factory audit
- **Data-Driven**: Automatic recording of filling volume per machine, MES upload
- **Cost Reduction**: Reduced 6-9 workers, saving hundreds of thousands annually
