## System Components

The automatic dough feeding system includes stacking modules, dough shaping mechanisms, buffer conveyors, incline conveyors, cart rail systems, control systems, and monitoring systems.

### Dough Stacking Module
Dough sheets from the sheeter are conveyed to the stacking module for initial forming, then shaped by rollers for standardized dimensions.

### Buffer Conveying Module
Bridges the gap between dough sheet conveying and cart pickup, maintaining sufficient dough inventory for continuous operation.

### Cart Rail System
The filling rail's transverse support extends outward with additional support legs, creating a layered compact layout for the dough rail.

### Material Standards
- All frames, rollers, and shafts in food-grade 304 stainless steel
- White PU conveyor belts
- Compliant with food safety standards

### ZGM-V3.0 Hybrid Flexible Feeding System

Borica's proprietary ZGM-V3.0 scheduling software, complementary to the ZGL-V3.0 filling system:
- Real-time data monitoring for dynamic feeding matching production demand
- Flexible parameter settings, one-button start/stop
- Production data tracking: records line-wide and per-machine output
- Manual mode available
- Zero-delay sensor data acquisition, auto-updating cart positioning

### Smart Cart (Core)

- 304 stainless steel suspended frame, fully enclosed sheet metal
- Conveyor belt rated ≥100kg, food-grade white PU material
- Large-diameter stainless steel wheels with rubber coating, servo drive
- Dual sensor + mechanical hard stop redundant safety
- Multi-point sensors for precise dough block positioning and spacing

### Safety & Hygiene Design

- 3-color indicator light per station (green/yellow/flashing red)
- Impact guard net protecting maintenance personnel below
- Built-in rail dust removal system reducing cleaning labor
- No complex multi-belt design eliminating dead corners
- Servo cart requires no gear oil, zero leakage risk

## Product Advantages (vs. Traditional Belt Conveying)

| Aspect | Traditional Belt | Borica ZGM-V3.0 System |
|--------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Space | Large, multiple nodes | Compact, standardized |
| Failure Risk | Many belt joints | Modular, fewer failure points |
| Hygiene | Hard-to-clean dead corners | No complex belts, no dead corners |
| Oil Leakage | Gear motor oil leaks | Servo drives, zero leakage |
| Control Accuracy | Poor synchronization | Servo closed-loop, S-curve zero-impact |
| Noise | High noise | Low noise operation |
| Energy | High idle loss | Single servo + low-friction rail |
| Dough Quality | Stacking/pressing/deformation | Zero-impact, gluten structure preserved |
| Expandability | Long modification downtime | Reserved interface for easy expansion |
| Relocation | High disassembly loss | 100% modular reuse |
